WHO WE ARE

A do tank.
Not a think tank.

Netratvshaala is a leadership and innovation platform working across governance, grassroots entrepreneurship, and emerging technologies. We partner with governments, institutions, and ecosystem leaders to build solutions that translate into real-world impact.

We work with governments, police departments, startups, MSMEs, women entrepreneurs, content creators, and public officials. We have shaped startup policy, trained police on AI threats, powered women founders, and anchored national conversations on inclusive technology.

"Every problem India faces is, at its root, a leadership problem."

THEORY OF CHANGE

We don't just train leaders.
We build the architecture for change.

01
Identify the Leadership Gap
Every sector in India — governance, tech, culture, entrepreneurship — has leadership gaps. We identify where the gap is widest and where intervention will have the highest multiplier impact.
02
Design the Right Program
For each gap we design a specific, structured program — not a generic workshop, but a deeply researched intervention with measurable outcomes, clear mentorship tracks, and institutional partnerships.
03
Build the Ecosystem Around It
Programs alone don't create change. We build the ecosystem — connecting participants with mentors, investors, government bodies, incubators, and peer networks that sustain impact long after the program ends.
04
Scale Through Institutional Partnerships
We scale proven programs through government mandates, CSR partnerships, and bilateral collaborations. What starts in one district can become a national movement — as Yashoda AI did, from UP villages to 17 states.
05
Measure, Document, Iterate
We track outcomes — not just outputs. Leaders trained, businesses started, policies shaped, lives changed. Every program is evaluated, refined, and scaled on the basis of real-world results.

"From local action to national impact — that is the Netratvshaala way."

OUR JOURNEY · 2016 — 2026

A decade of planting seeds.
A movement in the making.

2016
Youth Ideas — The First Dialogue
A small circle of young people in UP sat in unscripted conversation with IAS officers and public leaders. No agenda. No complaints. Just: "What can we build?" It was the seed of everything that followed.
GOVERNANCE
2018
Campus Leadership Labs Across UP
Thousands of college students across Uttar Pradesh experienced a new way of seeing themselves — not as bystanders, but as problem-solvers. Future founders, officers, and community leaders passed through our workshops.
EDUCATION
2020
Online Leadership Camps — Constraint Becomes Reach
When the world locked down, we opened wider. Digital bootcamps and dialogue series connected young people, policymakers, and practitioners across geographies. Our reach expanded beyond UP for the first time.
DIGITAL
2021
AARAMBH — Startup Bootcamp with Govt of UP
In partnership with the Government of Uttar Pradesh's "Start in UP" initiative, we launched AARAMBH — a state-backed startup bootcamp taking early-stage founders from idea to pilot, connecting them to incubators and state support systems.
STARTUP · GOVT PARTNERSHIP
2022
Investor Workshops + Hackathons for Smart City Solutions
We demystified startup funding through investor awareness workshops and ran hackathons in five incubators, in partnership with smart city and state government — helping student teams and founders translate ideas into working prototypes with access to real infrastructure.
STARTUP ECOSYSTEM · SMART CITY
2022
Science for Policy — Open Access & National Vision
We launched the Science for Policy forum, contributing to India's "One Nation One Subscription" vision — democratising access to scientific research for every citizen and researcher across India.
POLICY · OPEN SCIENCE
2023
Women Entrepreneurship Program
We built a dedicated track for women founders — helping them formalise businesses, access networks, and step confidently into the formal startup ecosystem. India's next wave of entrepreneurs is female.
WOMEN LEADERSHIP
2025
Deepfake Detection & AI Awareness — Shrawasti Police
Netratvshaala's Inclusive AI unit partnered with Shrawasti Police to run India's most comprehensive deepfake detection and AI cyber-awareness campaign — making Shrawasti the first district in India to achieve this milestone. The campaign was featured in the UN Women AI Impact Summit playbook. In 2025, SP Ghanshyam Chaurasia received the Silver Polaris Award from Capital Media Group, London — an international recognition for the program's impact.
AI SAFETY · UN WOMEN · POLARIS AWARD 2025
2025
Digital Arrest Awareness — UP Police Training
We trained UP Police officials on digital arrest awareness — building their capacity to understand, investigate, and counter digital fraud, online impersonation, and cyber-enabled crimes affecting citizens.
DIGITAL SAFETY · LAW ENFORCEMENT
2025
Yashoda AI — National Commission for Women, Uttar Pradesh
In one of our most significant deployments, Netratvshaala supported the implementation of Yashoda AI with the National Commission for Women in Uttar Pradesh — bringing AI-powered tools to the frontlines of women's safety, grievance redressal, and empowerment at scale. Yashoda AI is now an active, ongoing program of Netratvshaala.
AI FOR WOMEN · NCW · ACTIVE PROGRAM
2026
Inclusive AI at Impact AI Summit, Delhi
Netratvshaala's Inclusive AI team participated in the AI Impact Summit, advancing conversations on inclusive AI and building stronger linkages between policy, innovation, and real-world implementation.

PROGRAMS & INITIATIVES

Six programs.
One purpose.

Each program at Netratvshaala is designed around a specific leadership gap — in the startup ecosystem, in public institutions, in culture, and in the digital age. Together they form a complete architecture for change.

01 — STARTUP CHALLENGE
₹5 Crore. One Idea. Unlimited Impact.
Funding · Mentorship · Ecosystem Access
We invest ₹5 Crore in breakthrough startup ideas — solutions to India's hardest problems in governance, health, agriculture, education, safety, or sustainability. This is not a grant. It is a bet on the leader behind the idea.
  • Open to early and growth-stage founders
  • Focus on deep-problem, public-interest sectors
  • Mentorship from ecosystem leaders + government advisors
  • Linkage to incubators, angels, VCs, global mentors and state support
02 — AI SAKSHAM: AI FOR MSME
Don't Fear AI. Lead With It.
AI Adoption · Hands-on Workshops · Peer Learning
India's 63 million MSMEs are watching the AI revolution from the sidelines. AI Saksham is a hands-on adoption journey that puts practical AI tools directly in the hands of small business owners and their teams — turning AI from a threat into their sharpest competitive edge.
  • Practical AI literacy for business owners and managers
  • Use-case identification for time and cost savings
  • Sector-specific tracks: manufacturing, retail, agri, services
  • Peer cohorts with fellow MSME founders across India
03 — BHARAT DEEP TECH: SCIENTISTS TO FOUNDERS
Turning Scientists into Founders. Researchers into Leaders.
Deep Tech · Scientist-to-Founder · Venture Building
India produces world-class researchers, scientists, and engineers. But brilliant research locked in labs does not change lives. This program bridges the gap — transforming deep tech talent into founders who can build ventures, attract investment, shape policy, and take India's technology capabilities to the world.
  • Scientist-to-founder transition: from lab to market
  • Venture building for researchers and engineers
  • Investor readiness and deep tech fundraising
  • Policy navigation and government-sector engagement
ACTIVE · NCW
04 — YASHODA AI
India's First National AI Literacy Movement for Women.
Women · AI Literacy · NCW · 17 States
Launched by the National Commission for Women (NCW) and powered by Netratvshaala, Yashoda AI is India's pioneering initiative to bridge the gender divide in AI awareness and digital empowerment. Not just a program — a transformative national movement equipping women from every corner of India with the skills, tools, and confidence to navigate, shape, and lead in an AI-driven world.
  • 5,539+ women trained across 17 states & union territories
  • 38+ workshops held in regional languages — Hindi, English & more
  • AI literacy workshops, curriculum tracks & AI Sakhi peer support
  • AI Squad Program: campus clubs nurturing youth AI leaders
  • Featured in India AI Mission / MeitY's AI by HER global challenge
  • Partners: NITI Aayog, WEP, Future Shift Labs & 15+ implementation partners
05 — INCLUSIVE AI
Making AI understandable, equitable, and usable in public systems.
AI Literacy · AI Safety · Governance · Public Systems
Inclusive AI is Netratvshaala's core pillar enabling responsible AI in governance and public institutions. We work with police, administrators, and policymakers to build capacity and deploy solutions that address deepfakes, digital fraud, and algorithmic risks. Visit Program Website →
  • AI literacy and digital safety for government officials
  • Deepfake detection and information disorder response
  • Digital arrest awareness and cybercrime capacity building
  • AI governance frameworks for public institutions
06 — SUSHMA SWARAJ MEMORIAL FELLOWSHIP
Telling India's Story to the World.
Content Creators · Soft Power · India's Voice
Inspired by Smt. Sushma Swaraj — India's most beloved diplomat, who wielded soft power like a superpower — this fellowship supports content creators showing India's civilisational depth, democratic values, and cultural strength globally.
  • Fellowship for India's soft power storytellers
  • Mentorship from journalists, diplomats, filmmakers
  • Platforms and amplification for Fellows' work
  • Annual cohort with national and international reach
